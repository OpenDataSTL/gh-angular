The following are guidelines for going to court. These are not court-specific, and rules for attendance may vary from court to court.

Legal Advice

While not required, it can be helpful to have an attorney represent you in court. There are several options for pro bono or discounted services to be found here: http://missourilawyershelp.org/find-lawyer/discounted-pro-bono-services/

Court Fees & Fines

In addition to the fine associated with your violation, cases may incur additional court fees. These vary by court based on the municipality's legal ordinances.

Dress Code

Appropriate clothing should be worn when going to court, though some courts do not require a strict dress code.

Do's & Don'ts

Electronic Devices

Cell phones, tablets, pagers, and any hand-held electronic devices brought into court with you should be turned to silent while you are inside the courtroom.

Smoking and Vaporizers

Smoking and the use of electronic vaporizers is prohibited inside court.
