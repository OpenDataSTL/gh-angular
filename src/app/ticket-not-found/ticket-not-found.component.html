What this means

You may still have pending court cases, or you entered your information incorrectly.

If you just received your ticket

If you just received your ticket, it may not have been entered into the system yet. Please check back soon. Tickets have to go through an approval process before being entered into the ticket database.

If you have missed your court date

This website shows only upcoming court dates. If your court date has passed and you have not resolved your case, there may be a warrant issued for you. If you have missed the court date for a case involving a warrant and the court date is in the past, it will not show up in our system. If you know this is your situation, you make look up the court where you missed your date using our court finder.
