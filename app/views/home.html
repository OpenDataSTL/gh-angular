Help

Whether it's court information, assistance with your case, questions related to reinstating a drivers license, or signing up for and carrying out community service to pay for your fines and fees, we can help! Search for your ticket and browse this website to learn more. If your ticket information cannot be found here, it may be that the municipality that issued the ticket is not yet participating in YourSTLCourts and you must speak directly with that court.

Tickets

Do you have a ticket but can't remember how much you owe, when your court date is, or whether you can pay it online? Are you trying to take care of a lost ticket or forgotten warrant? Do you need to find out who to talk to about your ticket without fear of arrest? If so, you are in the right place! YourSTLCourts can help you resolve your ticket or warrant or connect you to someone who can help.

About

YourSTLCourts, a MacArthur Foundation-funded project, seeks to provide a unified, user-friendly municipal court case and information lookup for the St. Louis region's many courts. The fact that we have so many political jurisdictions means that it can be confusing, difficult, and expensive to figure out how to take care of business. Many municipalities recognize this and have been partners in putting this system together. If the municipality you are trying to work with isn't here, let them know that you'd like them to participate!
